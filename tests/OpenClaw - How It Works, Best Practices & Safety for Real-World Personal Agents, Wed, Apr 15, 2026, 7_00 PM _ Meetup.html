Details

In this talk we'll go over the main building blocks behind personal agents like OpenClaw and how they fit together in practice. From there, we'll look at best practices for making them actually useful, and the safety patterns that matter when you run them in the real world.

About the speaker:
Maxim is a software engineer with 10+ years across web, cloud, and security. Early contributor to OpenClaw. Currently building AI tooling.
